    Part 6: Memo Completion Strategy¬∂

    üéØ MEMO-SPECIFIC APPROACH:

1. IDENTIFY MEMO ELEMENTS (10 seconds)
   - TO/FROM/DATE/RE fields
   - Formal vs. informal tone
   - Department/company context

2. ANALYZE BLANK CONTEXT (25 seconds per blank)
   - Business logic and flow
   - Formal language patterns
   - Policy/procedure language

   - Is the tone consistent?
   - Does it make business sense?

    Part 7: Policy Comprehension Strategy¬∂

    üìù POLICY-FOCUSED READING:

1. SCAN FOR STRUCTURE (20 seconds)
   - Policy purpose and scope
   - Effective dates and deadlines  
   - Action items and requirements

   - Who is affected?
   - What changes are being made?
   - When do changes take effect?
   - What are the consequences?

3. UNDERSTAND IMPLICATIONS (30 seconds)
   - What does this mean for employees?
   - What actions are required?
   - What happens if not followed?
