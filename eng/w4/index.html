    TUẦN 4 (PHIÊN BẢN CHI TIẾT): ASKING FOR INFORMATION (CÁCH ĐẶT CÂU HỎI)¶

    Việc đặt câu hỏi đúng không chỉ giúp bạn lấy được thông tin mà còn thể hiện trình độ Anh ngữ và sự tinh tế trong giao tiếp. Đối với một DevOps Engineer, đây là kỹ năng sống còn để làm rõ yêu cầu, gỡ lỗi (troubleshoot) và hợp tác hiệu quả.

    PHẦN 1: YES/NO QUESTIONS (CÂU HỎI CÓ/KHÔNG)¶

    Đây là loại câu hỏi đơn giản nhất, câu trả lời thường bắt đầu bằng Yes hoặc No.

    1.1. Cấu trúc nền tảng Công thức chung là đảo trợ động từ (Auxiliary Verb) lên trước chủ ngữ (Subject). Trợ động từ + Chủ ngữ + Động từ chính + ... ?

    Trợ động từ Ví dụ
    Do/Does (Thì hiện tại đơn) Do you like coffee? / Does he work here?
    Did (Thì quá khứ đơn) Did you finish the task?
    Am/Is/Are (Thì hiện tại tiếp diễn/Câu với "to be") Are you busy? / Is the server running?
    Was/Were (Thì quá khứ tiếp diễn/Câu với "to be") Was the system down yesterday?
    Have/Has (Thì hiện tại hoàn thành) Have you pushed the code yet?
    Will/Shall (Thì tương lai đơn) Will you join the meeting tomorrow?
    Can/Could/May (Động từ khuyết thiếu) Can you help me? / Could you explain that again?
    Should/Would (Động từ khuyết thiếu) Should I update the documentation? / Would you like some tea?

    1.2. Patterns theo cấp độ và ngữ cảnh

    • Cấp độ 1: Basic / Informal (Cơ bản / Thân mật)

    • Are you okay?

    • Do you have a minute?
    • Is this your book?
    • Can I call you back?
    • Did you see my message?
    • Are you coming to the party?
    • Do you need any help?
    • Is it raining outside?
    • Have you eaten lunch?

    • Can you pass me the salt?

    • Cấp độ 2: Standard / Neutral (Tiêu chuẩn / Trung tính)

    • Are you available for a quick chat?

    • Do you have any questions?
    • Is this seat taken?
    • Have you received my email?
    • Did you get a chance to review the document?
    • Should we schedule a follow-up meeting?
    • Will the report be ready by 5 PM?
    • Is there anything else I can help you with?
    • Does this solution meet the requirements?

    • Are there any objections to this proposal?

    • Cấp độ 3: Formal / Polite / Indirect (Trang trọng / Lịch sự / Gián tiếp)

    • Đây là kỹ năng cực kỳ quan trọng trong môi trường chuyên nghiệp để không bị coi là thô lỗ hoặc quá trực diện.

    • Thay vì hỏi trực tiếp, hãy dùng các cụm từ dẫn dắt:

    • Could you tell me if the new feature is deployed? (Thay vì: Is the new feature deployed?)

    • Do you know if the client has approved the design? (Thay vì: Has the client approved the design?)
    • I was wondering if you have a moment to discuss the project plan.
    • Would you mind telling me if this is the final version of the report?
    • I'd like to know if it's possible to postpone the deadline.
    • Do you happen to know if Mr. Smith is in his office?
    • Could you please confirm if you will be attending the conference?
    • I was hoping you could tell me if this approach is feasible.
    • Would it be possible for you to check if the server is responding?

    • I'm not sure if this is the right time to ask, but are you free for a call later?

    • Cấp độ 4: IELTS 7.0+ & Advanced Conversation

    • Sử dụng câu hỏi Yes/No để dẫn dắt, làm rõ, hoặc thể hiện sự tương tác trong thảo luận (đặc biệt là Part 3).
    • Tag Questions (Câu hỏi đuôi): Dùng để xác nhận thông tin hoặc tìm kiếm sự đồng tình.
      1. The deployment process is fully automated, isn't it?
      2. You've worked with AWS before, haven't you?
      3. This seems like the most logical solution, doesn't it?
      4. He won't be joining the meeting, will he?
    • Negative Questions (Câu hỏi phủ định): Dùng để thể hiện sự ngạc nhiên hoặc để đưa ra một gợi ý.
      1. Don't you think we should run more tests before deploying? (Gợi ý)
      2. Isn't it a bit risky to push this change on a Friday? (Thể hiện sự quan ngại)
      3. Haven't you finished the ticket yet? (Thể hiện sự ngạc nhiên)
    • Clarifying Questions (Câu hỏi làm rõ):
      1. So, are you saying that we need to rewrite the entire module?
      2. If I understand correctly, are you suggesting we switch to a different cloud provider?

    PHẦN 2: WH- QUESTIONS (CÂU HỎI DÙNG TỪ ĐỂ HỎI)¶

    Đây là loại câu hỏi dùng để lấy thông tin cụ thể, không thể trả lời bằng Yes/No.

    2.1. Cấu trúc nền tảng Từ để hỏi (Wh-word) + Trợ động từ + Chủ ngữ + Động từ chính + ... ?

    2.2. Patterns chi tiết theo từng Từ để hỏi (Wh-word)

    A. WHAT (Cái gì, Gì)

    • Basic / Informal:
    • What's your name?
    • What's this?
    • What time is it?
    • What are you doing?
    • What happened?
    • Professional / DevOps Context:
    • What are the project requirements?
    • What is the deadline for this task?
    • What seems to be the problem?
    • What is the root cause of this bug?
    • What steps did you take to reproduce the error?
    • What tools are we using for this project?
    • What is the current status of the deployment?
    • What are the next steps?
    • What would be the ideal outcome?
    • What are the potential risks of this approach?
    • Advanced / Indirect:
    • Could you tell me what the main objective is?
    • I'd like to understand what led to this decision.
    • Can you clarify what you mean by "technical debt"?

    B. WHEN (Khi nào)

    • Basic / Informal:
    • When is your birthday?
    • When can we meet?
    • When did you get here?
    • Professional / DevOps Context:
    • When is the project kick-off meeting?
    • When can we expect the first draft?
    • When was the last successful deployment?
    • When will the server maintenance be completed?
    • When would be a good time to schedule a code review?
    • Advanced / Indirect:
    • Do you have any idea when the update will be released?
    • Could you give me a timeline for when this will be implemented?

    C. WHERE (Ở đâu)

    • Basic / Informal:
    • Where are you from?
    • Where is the restroom?
    • Where did you put my keys?
    • Professional / DevOps Context:
    • Where can I find the documentation for this API?
    • Where are the log files located?
    • Where in the code is this function being called?
    • Where is the project repository hosted?
    • Advanced / Indirect:
    • Could you show me where the configuration file is?
    • I was wondering where I could get more information on this topic.

    D. WHO (Ai)

    • Basic / Informal:
    • Who is that?
    • Who called me?
    • Who told you that?
    • Professional / DevOps Context:
    • Who is responsible for this module?
    • Who is the project manager?
    • Who has access to the production server?
    • Who should I talk to about this issue?
    • Who approved this change request?
    • Advanced / Indirect:
    • Do you happen to know who is leading this initiative?
    • Can you tell me who to contact for support?

    E. WHY (Tại sao)

    • Lưu ý: "Why" có thể mang tính chất vấn, nên cẩn thận khi dùng trong môi trường chuyên nghiệp.
    • Basic / Informal:
    • Why are you laughing?
    • Why is the sky blue?
    • How come you didn't call me? ("How come" là cách nói thân mật của "Why", không cần đảo ngữ).
    • Professional / DevOps Context (Sử dụng các cách nói giảm nhẹ):
    • What was the reason for choosing this database? (Lịch sự hơn: Why did you choose...?)
    • Could you explain the rationale behind this architectural decision?
    • I'm trying to understand why this error is occurring.
    • What's the purpose of adding this extra step in the pipeline?
    • For my own understanding, why do we need to use this specific library?
    • Advanced / Indirect:
    • I was wondering about the motivation for this change.
    • Could you walk me through the thought process that led to this solution?

    F. WHICH (Cái nào - Dùng khi có sự lựa chọn giới hạn)

    • Basic / Informal:
    • Which one do you want?
    • Which color do you prefer, red or blue?
    • Professional / DevOps Context:
    • Which cloud provider are we using, AWS or Azure?
    • Which version of the library should I install?
    • Of these three options, which one is the most cost-effective?
    • Which server is experiencing high latency?

    G. HOW (Như thế nào, Bằng cách nào)

    • How (Manner):
    • How does this feature work?
    • How can I fix this bug?
    • How do we optimize this query?
    • How can we improve our deployment frequency?
    • How + Adjective/Adverb:
    • How long will the migration take?
    • How often do you run backups?
    • How many virtual machines do we need?
    • How much memory is the process consuming?
    • How soon can you get this done?
    • How (Opinion/Suggestion):
    • How do you feel about this plan?
    • How about we refactor this code first?
    • What about using a different monitoring tool?

    TỔNG HỢP TUẦN 4: CẤU TRÚC VÀ TỪ VỰNG CHỦ CHỐT¶

    A. TỔNG HỢP CẤU TRÚC & MẪU CÂU CHÍNH¶

    • Cấu trúc câu hỏi Yes/No: Trợ động từ + S + V ... ?
    • Cấu trúc câu hỏi Wh-: Wh-word + Trợ động từ + S + V ... ?
    • Cụm từ bắt đầu câu hỏi Gián tiếp / Lịch sự:
    • Could you tell me if/what/where...
    • Do you know if/when/who...
    • I was wondering if/why...
    • Would you mind telling me if/how...
    • Do you happen to know...
    • Could you please confirm...
    • Câu hỏi đuôi (Tag Questions): ..., isn't it? / ..., haven't you? / ..., will they?
    • Câu hỏi Phủ định (Negative Questions): Don't you think...? / Isn't it...?
    • Câu hỏi Chuyên nghiệp (DevOps Context):
    • What are the requirements/the deadline/the root cause?
    • When will the maintenance be completed?
    • Where can I find the documentation?
    • Who is responsible for this module?
    • What was the reason for this decision? (Lịch sự hơn "Why")
    • How can we optimize/improve/fix this?
    • How long/often/many/much...?

    B. TỔNG HỢP TỪ VỰNG & CỤM TỪ HỮU ÍCH¶

    • Từ để hỏi (Question Words):
    • What, When, Where, Who, Whom, Whose, Why, Which, How
    • Trợ động từ (Auxiliary Verbs):
    • Do, Does, Did
    • Am, Is, Are, Was, Were
    • Have, Has, Had
    • Will, Shall, Would, Should, Can, Could, May, Might, Must
    • Động từ dùng để hỏi (Verbs for Asking):
    • to ask (hỏi)
    • to inquire (hỏi, trang trọng)
    • to request (yêu cầu)
    • to confirm (xác nhận)
    • to clarify (làm rõ)
    • to verify (xác minh)
    • to elaborate on (giải thích thêm về)
    • Danh từ chuyên ngành (Technical Nouns from Examples):
    • requirement (yêu cầu)
    • deadline (hạn chót)
    • root cause (nguyên nhân gốc rễ)
    • error / bug / issue / problem (lỗi)
    • documentation (tài liệu)
    • repository (kho chứa code)
    • deployment (việc triển khai)
    • maintenance (bảo trì)
    • configuration (cấu hình)
    • proposal (bản đề xuất)
    • approach / solution (phương pháp / giải pháp)
